This program is intended to help people who are colorblind determine if outfits match.
I personally am colorblind and have strtuggled over the years with picking matching outfits. 
I wrote this so that you are able to upload an image that is saved on your pc.
It prompts asking for the filepath. After entering it uses fashion industry and mathematical 
standards in order to determine if it clashes or is too close in hue or shade.
